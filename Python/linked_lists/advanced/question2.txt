Remove duplicates from an unsorted linked list
Source: https://www.geeksforgeeks.org/problems/remove-duplicates-from-an-unsorted-linked-list/1

Given an unsorted linked list of N nodes. The task is to remove duplicate elements from the linked list.

Example 1:
Input: LinkedList: 5->2->2->4->5->2
Output: 5 2 4

Example 2:
Input: LinkedList: 2->2->2->2->2
Output: 2
