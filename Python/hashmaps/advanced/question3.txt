Find the longest substring with k unique characters
Source: https://www.geeksforgeeks.org/find-the-longest-substring-with-k-unique-characters-in-a-given-string/

Given a string S and an integer k, find the length of the longest substring that contains exactly k unique characters.

Example 1:
Input: S = "aabbcc", k = 1
Output: 2

Example 2:
Input: S = "aabbcc", k = 2
Output: 4

Example 3:
Input: S = "aabbcc", k = 3
Output: 6
